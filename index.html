About me.

Hello.

I am a recent graduate of the Northcoders bootcamp and I am really excited to show you how far I've come in such a short period of time.

I started my journey as a software developer like most people did.
Fleeing France before they closed down the borders due to a global pandemic.

It was at this point that I decided I would make the most of this situation and apply myself in learning a new skill.

The first month of this endeavour was spent fumbling around on random online courses. After realising that I could truly make a career out of this I jumped in the deep-end and applied for the Northcoders Bootcamp.

Three months later and here we are!

A grand total of 4 months hard work in order to get the chance to be able to show off my humble website and body of work.

I really hope you all enjoy.
